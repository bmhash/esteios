# Fragments — Raw Notes Instructions

The `fragments/` directory contains raw notes, seeds, unfinished thoughts, and working material.

## Purpose

- **Private working space** — not published, not part of the book structure
- **Idea incubation** — thoughts that may become chapters or parts of chapters
- **Research notes** — observations, quotes, connections not yet formalized
- **Writing experiments** — trying out different approaches or phrasings

## AI Role in Fragments

- Help organize fragments by theme or circle when asked
- Suggest connections between fragments and existing chapters
- Help cross-reference fragments with source texts
- Never generate content — fragments are the author's raw thinking
- Respect the informal, unpolished nature of this space

## Working with Fragments

- Fragments don't need to follow chapter structure
- Can be in any format: lists, paragraphs, quotes, questions
- May be in Portuguese, English, or mixed
- May reference personal experiences not suitable for the book
- This is a thinking space, not a publishing space

## Privacy

- Fragments are excluded from publication via `.quartoignore`
- This is private material between the author and their process
- Don't suggest publishing fragments without explicit request
