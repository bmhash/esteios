# Meditations — Key Passages Index

Organized by theme for quick reference while writing Esteios chapters.

## Acceptance & Control

### On what is in our power
> "You have power over your mind — not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VII

### On accepting what happens
> "Accept the things to which fate binds you, and love the people with whom fate brings you together, but do so with all your heart."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VI

### On the obstacle
> "The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book V

## Mortality & Time

### On death
> "Think of yourself as dead. You have lived your life. Now take what's left and live it properly."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VII

### On the present moment
> "Confine yourself to the present."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VII

### On the shortness of life
> "You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do and say and think."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book II

## Virtue & Character

### On doing the right thing
> "Waste no more time arguing what a good man should be. Be one."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book X

### On opinion vs. reality
> "If you are distressed by anything external, the pain is not due to the thing itself, but to your estimate of it; and this you have the power to revoke at any moment."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VIII

### On inner citadel
> "Nowhere can man find a quieter or more untroubled retreat than in his own soul."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book IV

## Community & Others

### On interconnection
> "What brings no benefit to the hive brings none to the bee."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book VI

### On dealing with difficult people
> "When you wake up in the morning, tell yourself: The people I deal with today will be meddling, ungrateful, arrogant, dishonest, jealous, and surly. They are like this because they can't tell good from evil."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book II

### On kindness
> "Kindness is invincible."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book XI

## Perspective & Wisdom

### On the view from above
> "Look down from above on the countless gatherings and countless ceremonies, and every sort of voyage in storm and calm, and the disputes between those being born, living together, and dying."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book IX

### On simplicity
> "If you seek tranquility, do less. Or (more accurately) do what's essential."
> 
> — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*, Book IV
